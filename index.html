05 — Specification

Built for energy,
acoustics and comfort.

Technical ambition matches architectural ambition. Passive-house thermal concept, double-flow ventilation, reinforced acoustic detailing for airport proximity, photovoltaic readiness.

Heating & Cooling

Viessmann Vitocal 222-S reversible air-to-water heat pump per building. Integrated 250 L DHW cylinder. Underfloor heating on every level.

Ventilation

Zehnder ComfoAir Q350 double-flow mechanical ventilation with heat recovery. Silent operation. Continuous filtered fresh air.

Envelope

22 cm rock-wool external insulation. Ventilated brick cladding. Triple-glazed wood-aluminium joinery. Passive-house concept verified by blower-door test.

Solar Ready

Photovoltaic-ready roof. ECS solar-thermal provisions. EV charging point per garage as standard.

Acoustics

Reinforced acoustic detailing across all envelopes due to airport proximity. Multi-layer glazing. Isolated floor slabs between levels.

Wellness

Whirlpool bath in every unit. Two dedicated fitness rooms. Shared outdoor swimming pool with pool house at the heart of the plot.

08 — Tax Structure

The tax profile amplifies
the investment case.

Luxembourg tax rules for new-build rental residential property generate material deductions that reduce the borrower's effective tax bill and strengthen after-tax cash flows — particularly in the early years when DSCR is under pressure.

INVESTMENT UNITS — TAXABLE RENTAL INCOME, YEAR 1
Gross rental income (3 × €5k × 12) €180,000
Less: Operating expenses (~8%) −€14,400
Less: Interest on investment tranche −€100,750
Less: Accelerated depreciation (4%) −€105,300
Taxable rental income −€40,450

The negative figure is a deductible tax loss that offsets the borrower's other taxable income. At a 45.8% marginal rate, this represents a cash tax saving of approximately €18,500 in Year 1 — growing as depreciation is fully absorbed over the initial six-year period.

AFTER-TAX CASH FLOW — INVESTMENT UNITS, YEAR 1

In Years 1–2 the project generates ~€83k of positive after-tax cash flow on the investment units alone — before any sublease upside at the Anex operator level.

Additional Tax Benefits

Three further tax advantages attach to the project under current Luxembourg law, subject to confirmation by tax advisor at structuring.

€50k

TVA Logement (3%)

Reduced 3% VAT rate on construction costs attributable to the owner-occupied unit, capped at €50k refund. Cash saving recovered post-delivery via Administration de l'Enregistrement.

4% / yr

Accelerated Depreciation

Luxembourg accelerated depreciation on new-build rental residential — 4% linear over the first 6 years, applied to construction cost basis of the three investment units (~€2.6m).

100%

Interest Deduction

Interest on the investment tranche is fully deductible against rental income under the revenus provenant de la location de biens category. Residence interest deductible up to statutory personal caps.

Figures are indicative and based on current Luxembourg tax law (2026). Final structuring to be confirmed by a Luxembourg-qualified tax advisor (e.g. Atoz, Arendt, or equivalent) prior to drawdown. Depreciation rates, TVA logement mechanics and caps have been revised multiple times in recent years — current regulations apply.
